We are a group of environmental scientists and nature lovers determined to make environmental science understandable for the general public. TREES (Talks on REliable Environmental Science) is a student-driven scientific organization. TREES was born out of identifying a growing public interest in environmental topics, such as climate change and air, water, and soil pollution and yet, a lack of reliable information based solidly on science in easily consumable form.
